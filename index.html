School Programs

Explore and discover the technology behind building websites! Students will learn the basics of web development and basic web design frameworks - HTML & CSS - through creation of their own static websites. During the first 6 sessions, students will learn best practices through the agile workflow model used in tech companies from Amazon to Zulily. During the last session, students will present their website as a final project. Limited space - Only 12 spots available!
